Our Story

A Legacy of
Refined Living

Established in 2015, Korier Limited is a real estate company dedicated to providing modern residential apartments and townhouses in the best locations. Our flagship development, Soaring Court, deploys modern architectural designs with stylish interiors and marvelously built exteriors in a serene and cozy environment.

Situated in a residential enclave at Adenta with easy access to East Legon, Aburi, Madina, and West Legon — Soaring Court is a place for physical, mental, and spiritual tranquility.

Our Mission
To provide an outstanding level of finishing services in the best locations of the land.
Our Vision
To become the leading Real Estate Company in Africa and beyond, providing best-in-class services to meet the needs of our clients.
